# Offload Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** [DATE]
**Applies to:** offloads.io, the Offload app, and the Offload MCP server (offload-mcp.onrender.com/mcp).

Offload ("we", "us") connects people who need a digital task done with vetted human "Doers", with AI handling the automatable part. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. We keep it short on purpose.

## The one thing to know first
**We never ask for your passwords.** Doers do not get your account credentials. When a task needs account access, you grant scoped access through the proper provider or perform the one sensitive step yourself.

## What we collect
- **Task content you provide.** The task description, title, and any context you include when you request a quote or post a task. Include only what the Doer needs; do not paste passwords, full card numbers, or government IDs.
- **Contact details.** Email address, if you provide one, for task updates and the checkout link.
- **Payment information.** Processed by **Stripe**. We do not store full card numbers; Stripe handles card data under its own terms.
- **Account and usage data.** If you create an account: identifiers and basic activity needed to run the service (tasks posted, status, timestamps).
- **MCP server requests.** When you use the Offload MCP server, we receive the task description you submit to get a quote or post a task. The free quote tool requires no account or key.
- **Technical data.** Standard server logs (IP, timestamp, user agent) for security and reliability. [CONFIRM: log retention window, e.g. 30 days.]

## How we use it
- To produce a quote, scope the task, and match it to a vetted Doer.
- To process payment and hold it in **escrow** until you approve the delivered work.
- To send task-related updates and support.
- To operate, secure, and improve the service.
We do **not** sell your personal information. [CONFIRM]

- **The assigned Doer**, who needs the brief to complete the work. Doers are vetted and bound by confidentiality terms. [CONFIRM the contractual basis.]
- **AI processing** that triages and pre-does the automatable portion. [CONFIRM the AI sub-processor(s) used and that task content is not used to train third-party models, or state otherwise.]
- **Service providers** acting on our behalf: **Stripe** (payments), [hosting provider, e.g. Render / Supabase], and [analytics/email tools, if any]. They may process data only to provide their service to us.
- **Legal**: where required by law or to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of the service.

## Escrow and payments
Payments are funded into escrow via Stripe when you post a task and are released to the Doer only after you approve the delivered work. Refund and dispute handling are described in our [Terms of Service].
